Frequently asked questions

How do I book a property inspection? +
Open any property page and click "Book an inspection." Choose a date and time, and our team will confirm by phone or email.
Are your land listings verified? +
Yes — every land listing is checked against the relevant state land registry before it goes live, and physically inspected by our team.
How do I list my property with JC Enterprise? +
Register for an agent account, then add your listing from your agent dashboard. New listings are reviewed by our admin team before they go public.
Can I save properties to review later? +
Yes — create a free account and tap the heart icon on any listing to save it to your favorites.
Do you handle rent payments and service charges? +
We coordinate rent and service charge collection for properties under our estate management. Contact our office for the specific process on your property.
How do I reach the CEO's office directly? +
Call 0803 419 9497 or see the About page for direct contact details.
